Two Picture Foot Scanner for Arch Information

We have built the two picture foot scanner
for customers who would like to buy insoles and the right athletic shoes with arch
support. The second picture focuses on capturing foot arch and volume information.
This feature is available on our browser-based app. All the customer has to do is take a second picture using our simple instructions.

With foot measurements, arch, and volume information,
we can provide a robust recommendation for the right motion control or neutral athletic shoes,
and insoles too. We provide the user with a foot arch score (1-100)
that goes beyond the simple classification of pronated, neutral, and supinated foot. Insoles can be customized based
on the arch score.

Personalized shoe recommendation display

One unique recommendation algorithm takes footn
measurement data along with model & brand specific footwear data to provide a specific
size and fit score for each purchase.
Our unique fit score is a digital representation of
how you would physically try the shoe on. It specifically shows
the different fitting points that matter while selecting a
particular style of shoe.

Shoe Visualization ( Coming Soon)

Our first-of-its-kind browser-based AR solution
allows users to visualize how the shoe will look on their feet at the comfort
of their home. With a click of a button, you can experience how the shoe
looks on your feet - almost like looking down in a virtual mirror.
All of this is delivered through a browser experience and
well-integrated in the fitting piece. Currently limited to close
shoes and few pre-defined foot positions but the product is slowly evolving
to match a full-blown app experience.
